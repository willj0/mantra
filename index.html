about jenndog

Jenndog is an aspiring artist from Surrey, England who has made waves in the experimental hip hop scene through the use of his bold linguistic style and frequent merging of genres.

Jenndog takes pride in his art and makes sure that every piece of music he makes is as great as the last. His early tracks such as ‘sunday’ and ‘hive’ from his 2013 album ‘doris’ has solidified him as a staple in the emerging experimental hip hop scene as well as an insertion to people who are trying to succeed in the music industry.
