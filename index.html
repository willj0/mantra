about me

I am an aspiring artist from Surrey, England and I make experimental rap music from highly personal alt-rap to horrorcore. I like to experiment with different styles and sounds of music to create abstract and thought provoking pieces. My latest single ‘manta’ is very personal and deals with topics such as addiction, relationships and new found fame.
